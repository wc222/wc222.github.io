About

He is a PhD candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Associate Professor Ming-Chang Yang. His research focuses on computer systems optimization, specifically including large language model inference optimization and high-performance memory storage systems. His research has been published in top-tier conferences such as OSDI and SOSP.

He is on the job market for 2025, and is interested in research/engineering positions in AI Infrastructure/Systems.
